FAQ

Q: How do I add and manage my pet's profile?

A: Go to My Pets -> Add Pet to enter your pet's details like name, breed, age, and health info. You can manage multiple pets from the same account.

Q: How does the app help me care for my pet's health?

A: The app tracks vaccinations, medications, vet visits, and symptoms. It also sends reminders for checkups, feeding, and medication schedules.

Q: Can I share my pet's health data with my vet?

A: Yes. Under Privacy & Sharing Controls, you can securely share health updates and records with your veterinarian.

Q: What should I do in case of an emergency?

A: Use the Emergency Actions feature to quickly call your vet, send alerts to emergency contacts, or view nearby animal hospitals.
